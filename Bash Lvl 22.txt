#Bash lesson 22From over the wire challenge BanditA program is running automatically at regular intervals from cron, the time-based job scheduler. Look in /etc/cron.d/ for the configuration and see what command is being executed.In terminal:$ ls -l /etc/cron.d/$ cat /etc/cron.d/cronjob_bandit22$ cat: /etc/cron.d/cronjob_bandit22.sh$ cat /tmp/t7O6lds9S0RqQh9aMcz6ShpAoZKF7fgvPassword will appear.
