#Bash lesson 15From over the wire challenge BanditThe password for the next level can be retrieved by submitting the password of the current level to port 30000 on localhost.In terminal:$ cat /etc/bandit_pass/bandit14 #to get password then echo the password.$ echo "4wcYUJFw0k0XLShlDzztnTBHiqxU3b3e" | nc localhost 30000Or by telnet$ telnet localhost 30000Then introduce the password..Password will appear.
